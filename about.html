About AI Interview Platform

The AI Interview Platform is a web-based application designed to help candidates attend technical interviews online. The system generates interview questions using Artificial Intelligence, records candidate responses through the webcam and microphone, and stores interview details securely for HR review.

Project Objectives

  • Provide an online interview experience.
  • Generate interview questions using AI.
  • Record interview using webcam and microphone.
  • Store candidate responses securely.
  • Help HR review interview performance.

Key Features

Candidate Registration
Secure Login
AI Generated Questions
Camera & Microphone Support
Interview Result
HR Dashboard

Workflow

  1. Register or Login
  2. Select Interview Domain
  3. Start AI Interview
  4. Answer Questions
  5. View Result
  6. HR Reviews Candidate Performance
